Infrastructure

High class infrastructure with good workforce.

  • Knitting

    Our Knitting division is equipped with advanced Mayer and Cie circular knitting machines from Germany. Knitting division has the capacity to produce 3 tonnes per day. The fabric range includes Single jersey, interlock, rib, flatback rib, pointel & drop needle rib, mini/full jacquard fabric, auto stripes, waffle, airtex, 2thread/3thread fleece, terry, loop knit and pique.

  • Dyeing

    Our dyeing unit is situated in the outskirts of Tirupur and has the facility of doing sample programs & bulk production having automatic lab dip machines. Dyeing Of Fabric is carried out in Soft-flow machines of 250,450 & 600 KGS.

  • Compacting

    Compacting machines Tube-tex ( from USA) and Ferraro (from ITALY) with dryer machines. To achieve batch to batch consistency in shade, eveness, excellent feel and perfect shrinkage properties. Production capacity is 8 tonnes of fabric per day on an average

  • Manufacturing

    Cutting :
    We use both manual and layer cutting methods, which would vary according to the styles and fabrics. The section also has Band Knife machines. production capacity 5,000 garments regular styles.

    Sewing :
    With its wide range of advanced machinery & proficient labour capable of producing stylized garments with perfection & finesse, the sewing section has a hi-tech infrastructure. The production lines are fully integrated and operational with various types of powered sewing machines from Brother, Juki, Pegasus. 150 imported machines equipped with our sewing unit.

    Checking :
    With its wide range of advanced machinery & proficient labour capable of producing stylized garments with perfection & finesse, the sewing section has a hi-tech infrastructure. The production lines are fully integrated and operational with various types of powered sewing machines from Brother, Juki, Pegasus. 150 imported machines equipped with our sewing unit.
